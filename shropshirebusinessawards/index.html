Shropshire Chamber 2020 Virtual Business Awards.

Dear colleagues

As our country, society and of course businesses battle Covid-19, we continue to develop new ways of doing things in this new and challenging world. The value of physical interaction and socialising is being sorely missed and will be for some time to come, however we all need a light at the end of the tunnel, things to look forward to.

Shropshire Chamber Business Awards is and will always be the highlight of the business calendar, however with such a vast attendance we all agreed we should move it to June 2021. However it has never been more important to celebrate the extraordinary adaptability, innovation and success that is Shropshire business, we therefore make no apology for presenting to you the latest creative solution to recognise Shropshire business. On Friday November 20th we will still celebrate with you through the Virtual World as we hold the Shropshire Chamber Virtual Business Awards 2020.

Your Chamber hold a privileged position as we represent Shropshire firms and raise awareness locally, regionally and nationally. Together with our amazing partners the show will go on and we own the responsibility to make it happen.

Richard Sheehan
Shropshire Chamber CEO
